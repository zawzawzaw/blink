Set amidst the Indian Ocean, the Maldives is synonymous with
breathtaking seascapes, live coral reefs and pristine tranquility.
Jumeirah Dhevanafushi, which means "unique island" in Sanskritis
400km south of the capital city of Male, making the luxurious
remote atoll the idyllic setting for the property. A high standard
of resort luxury had already been established by the existing
properties. The challenge was not only to match those standards
but also to be able to uniquely distinguish itself amongst the other
luxurious properties and stand on its own merit apart from the stunning landscape. With the resort offering guests the choice of ocean over-water villas and beachfront villas in two very different locations and environments, with the nearest island offering logistical support over 10km away, this was clearly no
by-the-numbers design task.
