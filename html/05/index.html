BLINK Design Group is Asia-born and internationally acclaimed for creating distinctive, successful hotels and resorts for our clients.

We understand that first impressions last. We orchestrate the senses and interplay of visual elements to create captivating designs that are true to the dreams of our clients, the values and expectations of their guests, and the operational realities of the business.

Our collaborative approach is inspired by the spirit of the place itself, fuelled by the creativity of young and gifted voices, and guided by experienced leaders.
