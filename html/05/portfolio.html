our portfolio

All of our projects express the essence of BLINK — an intuitive interplay of elements that create inventive, timeless, and successful solutions for our clients and transformational experiences for their guests.

Our portfolio encompasses distinctive hotels, resorts, restaurants, clubs, and spas — from innovative urban midscale hotels to exceptional resorts in the world’s most desired destinations.

We approach projects holistically, from concept to completion, providing just the right mix of integrated services — master planning, building design, interior design, and branding design.

BLINK’s eye-catching aesthetics and clarity of vision have made us the design firm of choice for brands in countries that include China, French Polynesia, Indonesia, India, Kenya, the Maldives, the Philippines, Singapore, South Korea, Thailand, Turkey, and Vietnam.
